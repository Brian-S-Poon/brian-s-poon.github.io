An evening of Rachmaninoff's Night Vespers and Byzantine Sacred Music

Date: Saturday, June 14, 2025
Time: 7:00 PM – 8:35 PM
Location: St. Paul’s Greek Orthodox Church, Irvine, CA

A rare concert of Orthodox music with excerpts from Rachmaninoff’s legendary night vespers and sacred music from Byzantine and Slavic traditions. Vox Obscura will be joined by professional oktavist Alexander Mayang for this exotic, sacred journey to the origin of music and liturgy. Located in the gorgeous St. Paul’s Greek Orthodox Basilica in Irvine, CA.

Tickets will be on sale soon.
